My Hero!

A website where you can acknowledge the people who have improved your life!

All too often the people who shape our lives the most and improve our outlook go unrecognized for their hardwork. We often value the heroes we see on screen
or in comicbooks way more than the heroes we have in real life! While we want to be sure to make our fictional heroes proud, this is a website dedicated
to recognizing how hard their real world counterparts work! This website allows for people to commend their heroes and recognize their hardwork by using our suggestion form!
We than post our favorite heroes for the world to see!
